Twenty-20-20

Prolonged use of screens can lead to significant damage to the user's eyes when done for long periods of time (source). Twenty2020 is an in-browser wellness tool made to help you follow the 20/20/20 rule when online: every 20 minutes, look away from the screen at something 20 feet away for at least 20 seconds.
